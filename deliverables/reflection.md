The development of this project was a challenging but rewarding experience. 

At the beginning, I was motivated and interested in the idea of building a functional database-driven web application. However, as I moved into the implementation phase, I faced several technical difficulties, especially with PSQL Log In and database setup. These issues required me to solve problems carefully and seek support from different sources, including online resources (Reddit, Wikipedia, Google, Gemini, etc.) to continue progressing.

One of the biggest challenges was making the database tables connect correctly with the demo page and ensuring that the application worked properly. Although this process was frustrating at times, it helped me improve my problem-solving skills and better understand how databases and web interfaces work together.

Despite the difficulties, I was able to continue developing the project and complete the main structure of the application, including the database design and the initial statistics page. This experience taught me the importance of persistence, debugging, and adapting when technical problems arise.

Overall, this project allowed me to strengthen my understanding of database management, web development, and the importance of planning and testing during the development process.
